Simple Survival
Can you be simple?

In this game, you are stuck being in a box, your objective: SURVIVE AS LONG AS POSSIBLE!!! Move around and collect the orange blocks to gain some health back. Your score is the amount of seconds you are alive for. How long can you survive for?

Be simple?

Mission Impossible
How long can you run?

There is no end to this game, you keep jumping and try not allow the squares to kill the last circle. Your jump height slowly increases and the squares become slightly faster the longer you survive. Your score increases with each moment that you are still alive. At some point, the circle won't be able to run anymore.

Mission accepted?
